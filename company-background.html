Company Background

The Begether team is a group of engineers and UX experts. Begether is incorporated in Delaware, USA.

Begether was founded by former employees of Intel and Microsoft. They have a vision where technology makes people's social life better and more fulfilling, instead of making it worse. Begether builds products for real people, helping them to live their lives to the fullest. Our aim is that our social products support your social life in a way no other products do. To achieve this, we are continuously seeking to understand how people practice their social life and trying to support it with technology.
